About Main Market Onitsha

Connecting Nigerian buyers and sellers in one trusted marketplace

Our Story

Main Market Onitsha was founded with a mission to bring the vibrant commerce of Nigeria's largest market to the digital world. We recognized the need for a platform that connects buyers and sellers in a secure, efficient, and user-friendly environment.

Our platform enables small and medium businesses to reach customers across Nigeria, while providing buyers with access to a wide variety of products at competitive prices.

Our Mission

To empower Nigerian businesses by providing them with the tools and platform needed to thrive in the digital economy, while offering customers a seamless and secure shopping experience.

Live Video Calling

We're proud to offer real-time video calling between buyers and sellers! Our PeerJS integration allows you to:

  • Discuss products face-to-face with sellers
  • Get real-time demonstrations
  • Ask questions and get immediate answers
  • Build trust through personal connections

How Video Calls Work:

  1. Seller shares their unique Peer ID
  2. Buyer enters the ID in the video call section
  3. Instant connection via our free signaling servers
  4. Real-time communication begins!
